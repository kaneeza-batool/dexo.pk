Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Beautiful Spaces

dexo.pk was founded in 2021 by a small team of interior design enthusiasts who believed that every Pakistani home deserved access to world-class decor — without the import prices. We started with a simple Instagram page, sharing our curated finds, and quickly grew into a full-fledged eCommerce brand loved by thousands.

Our name — dexo — is a fusion of "decor" and "expression." We believe your home is an extension of who you are, and every piece you choose tells a chapter of your story. From minimalist Nordic vases to warm ambient lighting, we source and deliver pieces that resonate with modern Pakistani aesthetics.

Mission & Vision

How We Got Here

Our Journey

From a passionate side project to Pakistan's fastest-growing home decor brand — here's a look at the milestones that shaped dexo.pk.

2021
The Beginning

Founded as an Instagram-based curated decor page with just 5 products and a passion for beautiful homes.

2022
Going Online

Launched dexo.pk as a fully operational eCommerce store with 100+ products and nationwide delivery.

2023
1,000 Orders Milestone

Reached our 1,000th order milestone, expanded to 8 categories, and launched the Decor Sets collection.

2024
10,000 Happy Homes

Crossed 10,000 customers, introduced loyalty rewards, and began same-day delivery in Karachi.

2025
The Future

Expanding into Furniture & Statement Pieces, launching a mobile app, and opening our first flagship experience studio.
